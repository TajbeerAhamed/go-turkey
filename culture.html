Lets Explore Their Culture

Hot Air Baloon

Cappadocia is one of the most popular places for hot air balloon flights in the world, and it's because of its unique landscape and perfect weather conditions all year round. There are roughly about 260 flying days in a year in Cappadocia!

Traditional Food

Accordingly, a classic Turkish breakfast is generally comprised of black and green olives, cucumbers, cured meats, dips and sauces, eggs, fresh cheeses, fresh tomatoes, fresh-baked bread, fruit preserves and jams, honey, pastries, and sweet butter.

Mosque

The Blue Mosque in Istanbul, also known by its official name, the Sultan Ahmed Mosque. A functioning mosque, it also attracts large numbers of tourist visitors. It was constructed between 1609 and 1616

Towers

The tower, which is the tallest structure in Istanbul, is located on Camilca. Hill on the Asian side of the city. The 369-meter tower provides a unique view of the city and includes a restaurant and an observation deck.
